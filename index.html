Augmented Building Blocks

Micromundos is a computational construction kit for children to learn about dynamical systems through tinkering and physical play, develop creative problem-solving and bridge the digital and physical worlds.

There are low-cost laptops for children to learn computation, but they are based on the personal computer (individual) and the GUI model (graphical user interface, purely digital, general purpose).

Portable and low-cost devices are needed for children to develop creativity and systems thinking through Tangible-Computation based on a TUI model (tangible user interface, collaborative, hybrid physical-digital model of computation, special purpose).
